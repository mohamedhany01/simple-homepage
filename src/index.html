Okay, let's dive down more into my personality my friend. In my free time, I do lots of things watching movies, TV shows, and even anime, I also play video games. Why?

Movies are great. The visual effects, mysteries, actions, heroes, and ancient civilization. All these things do not exist in real life. So, why I don't love them.

TV shows are great, too. They are the long version of movies, with a big chance to understand more the content, like characters especially the heroes and main villains, character building, eras, ideas..etc.

Anime is Japanese art. Originally the anime is comics, but in Japan is called manga. Usually, manga drawing in black and white. If the manga become popular, a Japanese studio take it and converts it into an anime which is the animated version of the manga with colors, animation, voices, soundtracks.

Video games are the interactive version of movies and tv shows. You are the one that choosing the hero, Determining the path of the story, choosing the good or the bad side. In nutshell, you are the controller.
